About Anyess Global

Founded in 2018, Anyess Global is an MSME registered proprietorship business dedicated to advancing India's digital infrastructure. Based in Kolkata, we specialize in the wholesale trade and distribution of high-quality telecom equipment.

Our vision is to be a key player in the telecom supply chain, bridging the gap between world-class technology and local businesses. We are committed to delivering reliable products and exceptional technical services to our clients.
